What we do

One company. A growing family of products.

Labii Reatum operates as a holding company for the software we create. Rather than building for clients, we build for ourselves — focused tools that solve real, specific problems, brought to market under our own name.

01 — Design

Start with the problem

Every product begins with a clear problem. We map the workflow, design the experience and prototype fast, so the idea earns its place before a line of production code is written.

02 — Build

Ship reliable software

We develop our applications end to end — interface, data and infrastructure — on a proven, modern stack that lets us ship dependable software fast and iterate on how people actually use it.

03 — Operate

Own it for the long term

We don't hand products off. Labii Reatum runs, supports and improves every app in the portfolio — taking responsibility for security, uptime and the people who depend on them.

Our approach

Small studio, serious software.

We keep the company deliberately lean so we can move fast and stay close to the people who use what we make. A single, refined toolchain underpins every product — consistent quality, quick iteration, and software that is genuinely maintained rather than launched and forgotten.

Privacy and reliability are built in from day one. We collect only what a product needs to work, secure it properly, and are transparent about how it's handled.

About Labii Reatum

Labii Reatum Ltd is a United Kingdom–based software company and the parent organisation behind our portfolio of applications. We exist to turn well-defined problems into dependable products — and to own and operate those products for the long term, under one roof.

As a holding company, Labii Reatum provides the structure, infrastructure and standards that every app we release is built on, while each product keeps its own identity and audience. If you'd like to learn more about the company or a specific product, we'd be glad to hear from you.
